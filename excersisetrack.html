THIS ARE SOME BEST STRENGTH TRAINING EXERCISE..

DEADLIFT

Lifting a barbell from the ground to a standing position, engaging multiple muscles, especially in the back and legs.

SQUATS

Lowering body into a seated position and returning to a standing position to target lower body muscles.

BENCHPRESS

Pressing a barbell away from the chest while lying on a flat bench, emphasizing chest, shoulder, and triceps strength.

>
PULL-UPS

Lifting the body upward by pulling up on a horizontal bar, primarily targeting the upper back and arms.

PUSH-UPS

Raising and lowering the body by pushing up from and lowering down to a plank position, engaging chest, shoulders, and triceps.

PLANKS

Holding a push-up position with the body in a straight line, engaging core muscles for stability and strength.

THIS ARE SOME BEST STAMINA BUILDING EXCERSIZES

RUNNING/JOGGING

Running and jogging are excellent cardiovascular exercises that can significantly improve stamina. .

CYCLING

Cycling, whether on a stationary bike or outdoors, is a low-impact exercise that enhances cardiovascular endurance.

JUMP ROPE

Jumping rope is a simple yet highly effective exercise for building stamina.

SWIMMING

Swimming is a full-body workout that builds endurance and strength.

BURPEES

Burpees are a compound exercise that combines strength and cardiovascular training.

BOXING

Boxing workouts, including shadowboxing and heavy bag training, are intense and excellent for building stamina, agility, and coordination..
